Easy Strawberry Cinnonmon Pancakes

Shay Pressley Recipe

Ingridenents:

  • Pancake Mix
  • Cinnomon sugar
  • Strawberrys
  • Whipped Cream
  • Milk
  • Red food dye
  • Syrup
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Add 1 cup of your choice of Pancake Mix and 1/2 cup of milk or water to a bowl
  2. Mix the pancake mix and milk together throughoutly. Then add in 1 egg and 2 drops of red food coloring and mix throughoutly
  3. Take 6 strawberries and cut them into small pieces. Place them in the fridge until the pancakes are done
  4. Heat up a skillet with a little oil on medium heat
  5. Add in two scoops of the pancake mix into the skillet
  6. Cook each side until the bubbles are gone
  7. Sprinkle cinnonmon sugar onto each pancake and add whipped cream on the top of the stack of pancakes
  8. Take the strawbberies and sprinkle them onto the stack of pancakes
  9. Feel free to add in any other toppings and do not forget the syrup!

This is my favorite recipe because pancakes are easy to manipulate. You can use different ingridents to make all types of pancakes. So, you'll never have to worry about having a bland breakfast! Strawberries are delicious when they are riped.

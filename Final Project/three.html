Fiction vs. NonFiction

Fiction

Fiction is not based on real-life events, made-up stories. Literature created based on imagination such as science fiction, mysteries, fantasy, etc.

NonFiction

NonFiction is based on real-life events. Literature created based on facts such as biography, religion, business, history etc.

Vocabulary

Vocabulary is connected to comprehension. The more knowledge you gain with words, the easier it gets to read. One way to learn more vocabulary is to read or listen to books more often.

Reading Comprehension

We learn to comphrend reading in our early childhoods, that is when the complex cognitive learning process begins. When your learn to comprehend words, learning new vocabulary makes reading flow easier.

Fluency

The abilities to read at a accurate speed, phrasings and expressions.

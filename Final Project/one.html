Phonics

Phonics is the relationship between letters and sounds. It teaches people to be able to connect reading to sounds and sounds to spelling. Overall, it enhances the success of children reading skills.

Phonemic Awareness

Phonemic awareness is the relationship of people abilities to focus and manipulate phonemics in syllables and words. It improves the reading skills of children more than instructions which children lack in when learning to read. Phonemic awareness is the smallest unit of spoken languages.

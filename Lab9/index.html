Strawberry Cinnamon Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pancake Mix
  • Cinnamon sugar
  • Strawberries
  • Whipped Cream
  • Milk
  • Red food dye
  • Syrup

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of your choice of Pancake Mix and 1/2 cup of milk or water to a bowl
  2. Mix the pancake mix and milk together throughoutly. Then add in 1 egg and 2 drops of red food coloring and mix throughoutly
  3. Take 6 strawberries and cut them into small pieces. Take half of the strawberries and add them into the mix. Place the rest in the fridge until the pancakes are done
  4. Heat up a skillet with a little oil on medium heat
  5. Add in two scoops of the pancake mix into the skillet. Make sure to cook until the bubbles on each side are gone. When ready take them out and place them on a plate
  6. Optional: Cook other sides for the meal.
  7. Don't forget the other half of the strawberries! Take the strawberries and place them on top of the stack of pancakes
  8. Add cinnamon sugar onto each pancake and add whipped cream on the top of the stack of pancakes
  9. Feel free to add in any other toppings and do not forget the syrup!

This recipe is the best for the summer. You can add in other fruits and condiments to make other types of pancakes.
